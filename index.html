The Approach

I've been moving my whole life. Dance got me hooked as a kid, and it's what eventually led me to group fitness instruction. After training through UW-Madison's year-long Rec Sports program, I've spent years teaching in Chicago—expanding from that foundation into cycling, strength training, kickboxing, yoga, and competing in endurance events like Hyrox.

As a software engineer, I bring the same mindset to fitness that I bring to code: analytical, efficient, results-driven. No wasted time, just programming that works.

Beyond The Gym

When I'm not teaching classes, I work as a software engineer. I have a Master's Degree in Computer Science and love building elegant solutions to complex problems.

Outside of work and the studio, you'll find me cycling outside, performing with my salsa dance team, rock climbing, or hiking (especially if I'm lucky enough to be near a mountain). Before moving to Chicago, I spent time as a trekking guide for Quetzaltrekkers Guatemala, leading multi-day hikes up volcanoes and through rural villages—an experience that deepened my appreciation for endurance, adventure, and pushing limits.
